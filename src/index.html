We're here for you

Fendalton Medical Centre is a friendly general practice dedicated to providing high quality care to you and your family.

Our Services

Men's Health

Having an annual ‘warrant of fitness’ health check is a sensible way of keeping on top of any health issues such as blood pressure, cholesterol, heart problems, cancer or hearing difficulties.

Womens's Health

Women are encouraged to regularly have smear tests, learn how to perform their own breast checks, and monitor their weight and blood pressure. Our medical team can also assist with contraception, fertility and menopausal issues.

Children's Health

Our GPs have a special interest in paediatric care and work together with our nursing team to prevent and treat common childhood illnesses and health concerns.

Travel Health

Don’t let your dream adventure abroad be ruined by illness. Many countries have highly infectious diseases which are not present in New Zealand.

Skin Cancer Assessments and Surgery

If you have any concerning skin lesions or conditions, we have doctors available who have had specialised training in assessing, diagnosing and treating various skin conditions.

Specialist Assessments

Workplace Health and Injury Assessments, Company Medicals, Dive and Driver Medicals

Patient Enrolment

The Christchurch Doctors is a general practice contracted to Pegasus Health, which provides funding, subsidised care and special programs for enrolled patients. The doctors at The Christchurch Doctors are members of Pegasus Health.
